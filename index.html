Our Hybrid Model

AI speed. Human accuracy.

Technology drafts the note in real time; a certified scribe refines every detail. You get the best of both — fast turnaround and documentation you can trust.

01 AI

AI Listens

Secure, ambient AI captures the patient encounter in real time — no extra hardware, no workflow change.

02 AI

AI Drafts the Note

A structured clinical draft is generated instantly — organized into the SOAP format your EHR expects.

03 Certified Scribe

Human Review

A trained medical scribe reviews and refines every line for accuracy, nuance, and compliance.

04 Synced

Finalized in Your EHR

The completed note lands in your system — accurate and ready before the patient leaves the room.

Every note is human-reviewed — never AI alone.
