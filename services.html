End-to-End Documentation Support

Six services, one dependable partner

Each service is designed to remove a specific source of administrative burden — delivered by trained professionals and supported by secure technology.

Service 01

Virtual Medical Scribing

Trained remote scribes document patient encounters in real time using secure platforms, transcribing notes directly into your EHR. The result is less administrative work, more face-to-face time with patients, and consistently accurate records.

  • Real-time documentation during the visit
  • Lower clinician burnout and after-hours charting
  • Improved documentation accuracy
Service 02

Pre-Charting Services

We review and update patient records before each appointment — organizing medical history, lab results, medications, and prior visit notes. Up to 70% of the chart is populated with verified, up-to-date information before the patient encounter begins.

  • Maximizes valuable clinical time
  • Verified, current patient information
  • Fewer administrative tasks for staff
Service 03

Virtual Medical Assistant

A human-assisted, AI-supported solution that handles front and back-office tasks and patient interactions. Designed for physicians, hospital administrators, and telemedicine providers who want greater efficiency without adding to in-house staff.

  • Clinical workflow and scheduling support
  • Patient communication handled professionally
  • Reduced professional exhaustion
Service 04

Chronic Care Management

Structured, proactive support for patients with chronic conditions — combining remote monitoring, customized treatment plans, and coordinated care teams to reduce hospitalizations and overall healthcare costs.

  • Personalized, proactive care plans
  • Remote monitoring and coordination
  • Better outcomes, fewer hospitalizations
Service 05

After-Hours Calling (Transcription+)

Reliable patient support around the clock. Our hybrid approach pairs AI automation with human review to produce precise, compliant clinical documentation — with minimal administrative burden on your team.

  • 24/7 patient coverage
  • AI automation with human accuracy checks
  • Compliant, dependable records
Service 06

Offline & DataScribing Solutions

An AI-automated system paired with human expertise to transcribe and organize patient data — including converting paper notes into clean digital records. The result is improved workflow efficiency and higher documentation quality.

  • Paper-to-digital record conversion
  • Organized, structured patient data
  • Cleaner workflows across systems
